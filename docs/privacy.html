Mobile Staking Toolkit

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Mobile Staking Toolkit is a wallet-connected staking utility for Solana users. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app uses and how that data is handled.

Information used by the app

  • Public wallet addresses that you connect to the app
  • Public blockchain data needed to display balances, stake accounts, transaction history, cooldowns, rewards, and staking state
  • Local app preferences such as theme, selected explorer, and cached interface data stored on your device

Information the app does not collect

  • Seed phrases or private keys
  • Passwords or wallet secrets
  • Traditional personal account profiles unless you voluntarily provide details in a support request

How information is used

  • To load public on-chain balances and staking state
  • To build and submit wallet-approved transactions
  • To improve reliability and support debugging when you choose to share logs or support bundles

Third-party services

  • Solana RPC providers may process public blockchain queries required for the app to function
  • Wallet providers process transaction signing requests that you approve
  • Explorer links may open third-party sites when you choose to inspect transactions

Data storage

  • Cached app data and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Public blockchain activity remains visible on-chain by design

Your choices

  • You can disconnect your wallet at any time
  • You can decline to share debug or support bundles

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed in the Solana dApp Store submission for Mobile Staking Toolkit.
