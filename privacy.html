Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Staking Mobile Toolkit ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App may process:

  • Public wallet addresses / public keys you connect to use Solana functionality.
  • On-chain transaction or account data required to build and submit stake-related actions.

The App does not request or collect:

  • Seed phrases
  • Private keys
  • Wallet recovery secrets

2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Connect your wallet session
  • Display stake account information
  • Build and submit Solana transactions requested by you

3. Data Sharing

The App may communicate with third-party infrastructure necessary for operation, such as:

  • Solana RPC providers
  • Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter integrations

These providers may process network metadata according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

The App is designed to minimize retained personal data. Any local app data is used only for app functionality and user experience.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app integrity and user interactions. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the publisher through the support details listed in the Staking Mobile Toolkit listing in the Solana dApp Store.
