# Privacy Policy — Staking with Solana Mobile

**Effective date:** 2026-03-06

Staking with Solana Mobile ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App may process:
- Public wallet addresses/public keys you connect to use Solana functionality.
- On-chain transaction/account data required to build and submit stake-related actions.

The App **does not** request or collect:
- Seed phrases
- Private keys
- Wallet recovery secrets

## 2. How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:
- Connect your wallet session
- Display stake account information
- Build and submit Solana transactions requested by you

## 3. Data Sharing

The App may communicate with third-party infrastructure necessary for operation, such as:
- Solana RPC providers
- Solana Mobile wallet adapter integrations

These providers may process network metadata according to their own policies.

## 4. Data Storage

The App is designed to minimize retained personal data. Any local app data is used for app functionality and user experience.

## 5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app integrity and user interactions. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.

## 8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the publisher through the support details listed in the Solana dApp Store profile.
